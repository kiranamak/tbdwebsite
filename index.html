What we do

At Weaverbird.tech we work with small businesses and brick and mortar stores to design you a website to allow you to continue operating and maintaining connections with your community in an increasingly digital age.

Our offer

Creating and renovating websites for small businesses and brick and mortar stores

  • Design or renovate a website for your business
  • Help your business continue to enrich the community
  • Emphasis on graphic design
  • Great user experience to ensure your customers' satisfaction
  • Open communication and a seamless work process to fulfill your needs
  • Especially relevant during the ongoing pandemic

Our inspiration

Our business model and vision for out impact in the community

Want a new website to cater to our strange new reality? If you contact us with a request, we will work with you to design a website tht meets your specifications and needs for free. We want to help your business adapt to the new stay-at home world so that the community your business has formed can stay together even while apart.

Let us know if you think we are a good fit for you by emailing hello.weaverbird@gmail.com or filling out the form on the contact page.
